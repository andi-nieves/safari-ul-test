UNIONBANK INHOUSE INSTALLMENT

Consolidated Mechanics:

  1. Inhouse installment may be availed through the EasyTransfer (Balance Transfer), EasyConvert (Retail Conversion), EasyCash (formerly Charge for Cash), and EasyBill (Balance Conversion Program:
    • The EasyTransfer (Balance Transfer) Program allows cardholders to transfer their non-UnionBank Credit Card balances to their UnionBank Credit Card and convert it into installment.
    • The EasyConvert (Retail Conversion) Program allows cardholders to convert a straight retail transaction on their UnionBank Credit Card into installment, depending on their preferred term.
    • The EasyCash (formerly Charge for Cash) Program allows cardholders to avail of cash and charge it on their UnionBank Credit Card and then convert it into installment.
    • The EasyBill (Balance Conversion) Program allows cardholders to convert the total or a portion of their outstanding retail balance of their latest Statement of Account into installment.
  2. This facility is open to select UnionBank Credit Cardholders in active status and with good credit standing, and may be availed by contacting UnionBank’s customer service channels or through the UnionBank Online App.
  3. A minimum amount of P3,000 is required to avail of EasyTransfer, EasyConvert, and EasyCash. A minimum amount of P5,000 is required to avail of EasyBill.
  4. There is no maximum amount for EasyTransfer, EasyConvert, and EasyBill provided that the cardholder has sufficient available limit to cover interest charges and fees. For EasyCash, the cardholder may avail up to 80% of their available credit limit. If the Available Credit Limit (i.e. Cardholder’s Credit Limit less the outstanding balance at the time of approval) of the cardholder is not sufficient, UnionBank Credit Cards has the sole discretion to approve only a portion of the amount applied for.
  5. Processing fees will be charged to the cardholder for every approved availment starting January 1, 2022. This fee is non-refundable should the cardholder decide to terminate the installment:
    • EasyTransfer & EasyCash: P350
    • EasyConvert & EasyBill: P500

  7. For EasyTransfer availments, the cardholder may only transfer balances from another primary, non- UnionBank credit card that is under his/her name, to his primary UnionBank Credit Card. Balance Transfer from one UnionBank Credit Card to another is not allowed. Only posted transactions that form part of the cardholder’s Outstanding Balance on their non-UnionBank credit card at the time of application may be subject to EasyTransfer. In case the non-UnionBank credit card has existing installment transactions, only the monthly amortization posted will be included as part of the EasyTransfer Option.
  8. For EasyConvert availments, only straight transactions, either billed (appeared in the latest Statement of account) or unbilled are eligible. Transactions carried over from the previous statement are not included. The cardholder may convert a single retail transaction (billed or unbilled) of at least P3,000 (through the UnionBank hotline and online app) or multiple transactions (billed or unbilled) amounting to a total of at least P3,000 (through the UnionBank hotline only).
  9. The following transactions are not eligible for EasyConvert:
    • Cash advances;
    • Any installment transactions whether merchant installment or previously availed inhouse installment transactions;
    • Gaming transactions (transactions from casino/gambling including placement of wagers, purchase of lottery tickets, or other values in conjunction with any gaming or gambling activity);
    • Cryptocurrency transactions (transactions involving digital or virtual currency that can be used to buy goods and services, investments, or trading transactions);
    • Transactions from stock trading, foreign exchange trading, financial trading services, money transfers, remittance, and the like;
    • Quasi-cash transactions (mobile payment or mobile wallet transactions and online payment systems such as but not limited to GrabPay, GCash, PayMaya, PayPal, Coins.ph, and the like);
    • Business transactions (such as, but not limited to, direct or multi-level marketing, as well as from merchants specifically engaged in wholesale and distribution, payment services, and professional services); and
    • Interest charges and fees.
  10. For EasyBill availments, the outstanding retail balance for conversion does not include installment transactions, fees, interest charges, and cash advance.
  11. For EasyCash availments coursed through customer service with Manager’s Check as disbursement method, the requirements are as follows:
    1. UnionBank Credit Card, and
    2. Valid ID with picture and signature of cardholder.
    If Manager’s Check is for pick up by representative:
    1. Authorization letter indicating the name of the representative and signed by both cardholder and representative,
    2. Valid ID with picture and signature of cardholder and representative, and
    3. Photocopy of UnionBank Credit Card.
  12. To avail via UnionBank’s customer service channels, the cardholder may:
    1. Email callctr_promos@unionbankph.com, and/or
    2. Call the UnionBank hotline at (+632) 8841-8600.
  13. The cardholder will receive an SMS notification upon approval of their request.
  14. The monthly installment amount from the UnionBank Installment Program availed shall be considered as a regular transaction on the UnionBank Credit Card and shall be part of the Total Amount Due on the Statement of Account for the month.
  15. UnionBank reserves the right to approve or reject installment applications based on its internal policies or for reasons it deems justifiable.
  16. In case of pre-termination or cancellation of the UnionBank Installment, or delinquency of the account, the cardholder shall be liable to pay in full the unpaid principal balance of the Total Amount Due plus Cancellation Processing Fee amounting to 5% of the remaining principal balance or P500 whichever is higher. The cardholder also understands that the payment of the unpaid principal balance shall not be deemed to have been made until the interests have been covered. In case of special promotions wherein UnionBank gives the Cardholder special premiums (includes promotional items, rewards points, rebates, etc.), UnionBank has the discretion to charge the full monetary equivalent of the premium should the Cardholder cancel or pre-terminate the installment. The equivalent amount will be charged to the Cardholder’s UnionBank Credit Card account on the next statement date following the date of pre-termination or cancellation.
